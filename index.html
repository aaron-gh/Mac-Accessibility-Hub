Mac Accessibility Resources

Hello, and welcome to the Mac accessibility resources page. Here you will find download links and information on third-party addons for the Mac to make the accessibility just that little bit better. Resources are divided into catagories at heading level 2, with each resource at heading level 3. For more information on why this exists, and who made it, check out the about and contact pages above.

Productivity

Indent Beeper

  • Description: A small hammerspoon module to add indentation beeps to VoiceOver.
  • Author: Pitermach
  • Status: Supported
  • Download

Transpoon

  • Description: A hammerspoon module to quickly translate the last phrase spoken by voiceOver or the contents of the clipboard.
  • Author: Guillem Leon
  • Status: Supported
  • Download

System tools

Triggrd

  • Description: A tool to make automations based on system events.
  • Author: Guillem Leon
  • Status: Supported

tdsr

  • Description: A console screen reader for Mac OS.
  • Author: Tyler Spivey
  • Status: Supported
  • Download
